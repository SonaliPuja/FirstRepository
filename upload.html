Mindmeld Parser

Mindmeld parser converts the mindmeld training data into sandbox consumable format.


  • Upload file in zip format (less than 1mb)
  • Zip the domain folder and upload file, domains.zip-->domains-->domain1,domain2..
  • Train files should be named train.txt according to Mindmeld norms


(Currently we are supporting Faqbot only)
